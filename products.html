Colinked

We built Colinked to help corporates find the most exciting and relevant qualified startups. The platform allows for corporates and qualified startups to better connect and collaborate in one place. Through the use of AI & ML, corporates have an efficient, feature rich experience to meet their scouting needs.

Eamli

We’re building eamli, a tool to significantly drive down the cost and time required by SME’s to complete financial forecast, financial due diligence and company valuations. Using the power of cloud computing and machine learning, eamli is targeted at both founders as well as a white label product for financial firms. Launching in 2019.

Colinked Capital

Colinked Capital is a platform to better enable high quality tech scale ups and growth companies to raise equity finance from a network of professional investors including HNWI, VCs, family offices and sovereign wealth funds. The platform has also been built to help founders get investment ready quicker with a sufficient level of due diligence in place.
