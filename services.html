Our services are tailored to help advise and support for our founder, corporate and investor partners.

Discovery Days

Discovery Days are full day workshops which help businesses understand and solve the technical and commercial challenges they face. We're looking to work on interesting problems and have organised Discovery Days with FTSE 100s, SMEs and startups. If a problem is interesting enough, we may consider providing the resources to build it out with our in-house development team.

InvestReady

Our InvestReady service is designed to create complete financial models and due diligence reports for series A-C investment rounds. We've made it incredibly easy to create all the technical reports you need to pass due diligence and raise finance.

Learn More

Corporate Innovation Club

The Whitespace Corporate Innovation Club is a community of over 120 international corporates, NGOs and government bodies that share an interest in using an open innovation to help create new, different experiences and revenue models. Corp club members are generally senior representatives who either have a direct remit around driving innovation on behalf of their organisation, or are a key contributor to it.
